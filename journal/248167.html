Boston Skyline CDR

darxus
9/3/09 11:33 pm
http://www.chaosreigns.com/gallery/boston_skyline_cdr_1.jpg.html

Merged from 9 exposures at 2 stop intervals from 30 seconds, to 1/30th of a second, all ISO 800 and f/3.4 (thanks to sensesurfer's suggestion).

I did some google images and maps searching to find the spot I shot from. I half expected to find another photographer there. There were two with tripods and SLRs set up when I arrived.

I did some panorama sets, and an HDR panorama set. Hopefully I'll get those together soon.
