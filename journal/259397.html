      Definitions by which sexual preference can be considered racism:

      Racism:
      1 : a belief that race is the primary determinant of human traits and capacities and that racial differences produce an inherent superiority of a particular race
      No.
      2 : racial prejudice or discrimination

      Prejudice:
      2 a (1) : preconceived judgment or opinion (2) : an adverse opinion or leaning formed without just grounds or before sufficient knowledge b : an instance of such judgment or opinion c : an irrational attitude of hostility directed against an individual, a group, a race, or their supposed characteristics
      No.

      Descrimination:
      1 a : the act of discriminating b : the process by which two stimuli differing in some aspect are responded to differently
      Yes.
      3 a : the act, practice, or an instance of discriminating categorically rather than individually b : prejudiced or prejudicial outlook, action, or treatment
      No.

      So the only set of definitions for which sexual preference which varies statistically significantly by race qualifies as racism is the one comparing two individual stimuli - two individual people. And I would say the definitions involving entire categories of people (races) are the ones that are generally intended with the use of the word "racism".
