Check your credit. Every four months.

darxus
10/8/07 03:39 pm
AnnualCreditReport.com

This is the website through which the three agencies which are obligated to provide you with one free credit report per year do so.

I recommend staggering them so you are getting a free credit report once every four months. I also recommend spending the about $8 to get your FICA score, which is the part lending agencies care about.

To find this in the future: If you google search for "free credit report", the first hit is this website. The second hit is an ftc.gov page telling you to go to this website.
