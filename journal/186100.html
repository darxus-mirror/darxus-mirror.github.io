For some time I have had a single quote at the bottom of my main web page. My favorite. I just decided to look up what I changed it to, and when.

2001-04-19 "Every normal man must be tempted at times to spit upon his hands, hoist the black flag, and begin slitting throats."

2005-12-05 "Life is either a daring adventure or it is nothing at all." - Helen Keller

2007-08-10 "Where are you going and what do you wish?" - the old moon, to Winkin' Blinkin' and Nod

I find the time frames shocking. And I feel like I need to be more careful in my selection.

I am also very recently fond of this:
"For every battle there is a price to pay. Now pick up your teeth and go home." - no fear

Oh, and there's still this:
"Let's just say that if complete and utter chaos was lightning, then he'd be the sort to stand on a hilltop in a thunderstorm wearing wet copper armour and shouting 'All gods are bastards'." - The Color of Magic, Terry Pratchett

I love my quotes collection, which I have actively been building since I found the internet. It is generally agreed that it is a decent way to get to know me: http://www.chaosreigns.com/quotes/
